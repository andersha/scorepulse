# Privacy Policy

**ScorePulse** is committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We collect nothing.** ScorePulse does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your scores and settings remain on your device.

## Third-Party Services

ScorePulse does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Regulatory Compliance

Since we collect no data, ScorePulse is compliant with all privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and others.

## Open Source

You may reuse this code and data as much as you want, as long as you publish where you got it from.

Source code: [github.com/andersha/scorepulse](https://github.com/andersha/scorepulse)

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
